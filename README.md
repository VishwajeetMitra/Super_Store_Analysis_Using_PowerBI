# Superstore Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore Sales dataset. The goal is to analyze business performance, identify trends, and generate actionable insights across sales, profit, regions, and customer behavior.

---

## 🗂️ Dataset

* Source: Superstore Sales Dataset
* Contains:

  * Orders
  * Customers
  * Products
  * Sales & Profit
  * Regions & Segments
  * Dates (Year, Month)

---

## 📑 Dashboard Structure

### 1. Executive Overview

* KPIs:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Total Orders
  * Average Order Value
  * Year-over-Year Growth
* Visuals:

  * Sales by Year
  * Sales by Category
  * Top & Bottom Sub-Categories
  * Sales by Region

---

### 2. Time Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Yearly Growth Trend
* Sales vs Profit Comparison (Combo Chart)

---

### 3. Customer Analysis

* Total Customers
* New vs Returning Customers
* Sales by Customer Segment

---

## 📌 Key Insights

### 💰 Sales & Profit

* Total Sales: 2.33M
* Profit: 292K
* Strong YoY Growth (~47%)

### 📈 Trends

* Sales dip in 2024 followed by strong recovery
* Peak sales months: March, September, November

### 🌍 Regional Performance

* West region leads in sales
* South region underperforms

### 🛍️ Product Performance

* Top Category: Technology
* Best Sub-Category: Copiers
* Worst Sub-Category: Appliances

### 👥 Customer Insights

* 98% returning customers → high retention
* Very low new customer acquisition

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on new customer acquisition
* Optimize low-performing product categories
* Expand in underperforming regions
* Leverage seasonal trends for marketing campaigns
* Improve profit margins through pricing or cost control
